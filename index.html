Key Regulatory Milestones & Dependencies
Critical dates and supervisory events shaping the assurance calendar across FY2026–FY2028.
AML/CFT · FY2026 Q1
AML Programme Annual Risk Assessment
Mandatory annual refresh of the AML/CFT risk assessment. DIA expects documented evidence of board approval. L2 thematic on beneficial ownership sequenced ahead of DIA examination cycle.
CoFI · FY2026
Fair Conduct Programme — Full Operation
FCP must be fully embedded and evidenced. Board attestation required. FMA focus on product governance and distribution conduct. L2 compliance review H1 + L3 audit Q3.
Deposit Takers Act · FY2026 Q2
RBNZ Licensing & Deposit Insurance
DTA fully operative. Deposit insurance scheme live. RBNZ licensing obligations in effect. L2 controls testing commences Q1. Full L3 audit by Q3 FY2026.
Climate Disclosures · Annual Cycle
XRB CRD Mandatory Reporting
Annual cycle: L1 data attestation Q1 → L2 review Q2 → Board sign-off Q3 → External assurance Q3/Q4. FY2026 is first full mandatory cycle for Tier 1 banks.
CCCFA · Ongoing
Responsible Lending — Affordability Reforms
Post-amendment monitoring of affordability assessment processes. FMA focus area. Quarterly L1 monitoring; bi-annual L2 controls testing; L3 audit every 18 months.
FMCA · FY2027
FMA Conduct Supervision Cycle
FMA theme-based supervision expected in wholesale conduct and product governance. L2 thematic review Q2 FY2027 ahead of FMA programme. L3 audit sequenced Q4 FY2027.
Governance Framework & Activity Definitions
Plan ownership, review cadence, escalation protocol and definitions for all seven assurance activity types.
Assurance Activity Definitions
Controls Testing
Detailed testing of control design and operating effectiveness against defined standards. Typically quarterly or bi-annual. Generates control ratings and remediation actions.
Attestation
Formal executive or Board sign-off confirming compliance with regulatory obligations. Required for Board risk committee reporting and regulatory submissions. Annual minimum for all Tier 1 regimes.
Thematic Review
Deep-dive L2 review of a specific risk theme or emerging regulatory concern. Typically 6–8 weeks. Risk-based and forward-looking. Outputs include a thematic report with findings, ratings and recommendations.
Internal Audit
Independent L3 assurance providing rated opinions on governance, controls and risk management. Reports to Audit Committee. Rated findings with agreed management actions and due dates.
Monitoring & Surveillance
Continuous or periodic L1/L2 monitoring using automated indicators, data analytics and manual sampling. Feeds compliance dashboards and KRI reporting. Escalation triggers defined.
Compliance Review
Targeted L2 review of specific processes, documentation or obligations against regulatory requirements. Lighter than a full thematic. Often triggered by regulatory change, enforcement action or horizon scan.
Regulatory Engagement
Planned interactions with RBNZ, FMA, DIA, XRB or other regulators. Includes supervision visits, self-assessments submitted, formal consultations and regulatory update briefings.
Plan Governance Framework
OWNER
Chief Compliance Officer. Owns plan, approves all material changes, presents quarterly status to Board Audit & Risk Committee.
REVIEW
Quarterly Plan Review Committee (CRO, CCO, Head of Audit, BU Risk Leads). Reprioritise against emerging risk, regulatory change and resource capacity.
REPORT
Board Audit & Risk Committee — quarterly pack: plan status, findings heat map, overdue actions, regulatory horizon and upcoming supervisory visits.
REFRESH
Annual refresh each September incorporating RBNZ/FMA regulatory work programme, Internal Audit universe and emerging legislative change pipeline.
ESCALATE
Significant findings, regulatory events or plan material changes escalated to CCO within 48 hours. Regulatory breach protocol activated as applicable.
Regulators & Key Interfaces
RBNZ
Prudential supervisor. DTA, Contracts of Insurance, banking prudential standards. Annual supervision.
FMA
Conduct regulator. CoFI, FMCA, CCCFA, CRD. Theme-based supervision & entity monitoring.
DIA
AML/CFT supervisor. Annual reporting obligations, on-site examination programme.
XRB / MfE
Climate standards setter. CRD mandatory reporting framework and external assurance.
WorkSafe NZ
H&S regulator. PCBU obligations, notifiable event reporting, workplace inspection.
Commerce Commission
CCCFA enforcement, Consumer Credit, fair trading obligations and banking competition.
